Our Methodology

Alzheimer's disease and related dementia (AD/ADRD) is an irreversible and degenerative neurological condition that severely impacts cognitive functions. SafeCircle integrates AI and micro-radar technology into a mobile health platform to ensure patient safety and reduce caregiver burden. The system provides real-time alerts, emergency support, and continuous monitoring through an intuitive interface.
