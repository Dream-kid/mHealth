Our Methodology

Patients with Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementias (AD/ADRD) will be continuously monitored using a micro-Doppler radar. After data collection, a CNN+LSTM model will process the input. If the model detects wandering or falls, caregivers will be immediately notified. An iOS application will be used to update and view the patient’s current status.

The app includes features such as real-time monitoring, an SOS alert button, access to event-triggered video recordings, and a patient dashboard that displays historical health records. Figure 3 outlines the complete methodology, while Figure 4 highlights the key features of the iOS application.
