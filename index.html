Our Methodology

We continuously monitor patients with Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementias (AD/ADRD) using micro-Doppler radar. The data collected is processed through a hybrid CNN+LSTM model to detect wandering or falls. If an anomaly is detected, caregivers are promptly notified via an iOS application. The app supports real-time monitoring, SOS alerts, event-triggered video recording, and a dashboard for tracking patient history.
