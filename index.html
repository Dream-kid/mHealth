Our Methodology

Alzheimer's disease and related dementia (AD/ADRD) is an irreversible and degenerative neurological condition severely impacting cognitive functions. Our solution, SafeCircle, integrates artificial intelligence and micro-radar technologies within a mobile health platform. This comprehensive system provides real-time alerts, emergency communication features, and continuous monitoring to improve patient safety and caregiver efficiency.
